# /management-approval

## Purpose

Directors/management own the FS.

## Pack contents
- Draft FS + notes
- Representation / approval letter template
- Summary of significant judgments & estimates
- Open items accepted by management
- Compilation report (if compilation)

## Record
- Approver name/role
- Date
- Method (signed letter / email / board minutes)

Until recorded → do not mark **ISSUED**.

## Completion

**Done when:** approver, date, and method recorded on disk — no ISSUED without this.
