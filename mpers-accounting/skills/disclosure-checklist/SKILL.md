# /disclosure-checklist

## Purpose

Completeness of notes under MPERS S3–S8 and topic sections.

## Core disclosure packs (tick all applicable)

- [ ] General info, domicile, principal activities
- [ ] Basis of preparation & going concern
- [ ] Accounting policies (significant)
- [ ] Critical judgements & estimation uncertainty
- [ ] PPE movement schedule
- [ ] Investment property (if any)
- [ ] Intangibles (if any)
- [ ] Inventories
- [ ] Trade & other receivables (+ impairment)
- [ ] Cash & equivalents
- [ ] Trade & other payables
- [ ] Borrowings (terms, security, current/non-current)
- [ ] Lease commitments / finance lease liabilities
- [ ] Provisions
- [ ] Share capital / equity movements
- [ ] Revenue disaggregation if useful
- [ ] Employee benefits expense
- [ ] Director remuneration / key management (related party)
- [ ] Related party transactions & balances
- [ ] Commitments & contingencies
- [ ] Financial instruments / risk (as required)
- [ ] Events after reporting period
- [ ] Authorisation date of FS
- Comparative figures throughout

## Output
Checklist with Pass / Fail / N/A + pointer to note number. Failures block finalisation unless waived with reason.
