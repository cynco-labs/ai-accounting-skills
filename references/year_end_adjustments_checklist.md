# Year-End Adjustments Checklist

Run every item. Mark N/A only with reason.

## 1. Cut-off

- [ ] Sales cut-off: last invoices / goods delivery around YE
- [ ] Purchase cut-off: goods received not invoiced (GRNI)
- [ ] Cash cut-off: deposits in transit / unpresented cheques already in bank recon

## 2. PPE & intangibles

- [ ] Depreciation for full year / pro-rata additions
- [ ] Disposals: remove cost & accum dep; book gain/loss
- [ ] Capital vs revenue review for large expense items near YE
- [ ] Amortisation of intangibles (MPERS: finite life)

## 3. Working capital

- [ ] Inventory count & valuation; NRV write-down
- [ ] Prepayments (insurance, rent, software, road tax)
- [ ] Accruals (audit/tax fees, utilities, commissions, bonuses)
- [ ] Deferred / unearned revenue
- [ ] ECL / specific bad debt provisions

## 4. Financing

- [ ] Interest accrual on loans / HP / overdraft
- [ ] Principal vs interest split verified
- [ ] Current portion of long-term debt reclassification

## 5. Employment

- [ ] Accrued salaries / wages
- [ ] Bonus / leave provisions per policy
- [ ] EPF/SOCSO/EIS/PCB liabilities complete

## 6. Tax

- [ ] Current tax provision (from tax computation draft)
- [ ] Deferred tax (if required / material under framework)
- [ ] SST/other indirect tax reconciliations if applicable

## 7. FX & other

- [ ] Retranslate foreign currency monetary items at closing rate
- [ ] Provisions (legal, warranty, onerous contracts)
- [ ] Related party balances confirmed
- [ ] Subsequent events review (adjusting vs disclose)

## 8. Equity

- [ ] Dividends declared (timing: liability when appropriately authorised)
- [ ] Prior period errors (if any) per S10 — partner review
